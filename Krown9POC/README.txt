	The Concept of this application is show the List of Sports Leagues & Their Details. 
	User will be able to personalize by reordering / Deleting the League. i.e. Favourite / upcoming Leagues can be ordered & can delete Leagues which are not interested.
	This Application will show Leagues related to Soccer, American Football, Australian football, Rugby, Cricket, Tennis, Ice Hockey, Basketball, Motorsport, Golf, Baseball, Cycling, Fighting and many more sports.
	
	Information will be fetched from "www.thesportsdb.com" website. - https://www.thesportsdb.com/api.php
